About · Meet the Grills

Big Red, Monty & Nelly

Three specialized charcoal broilers do the work in our kitchen — each built for a different job.

{% include "_about_subnav.html" %}

“Big Red” — Woodshow Broiler

Built by J&R in Texas, Big Red is our kitchen’s primary grill. A wheel on the side raises and lowers the heavy cast-iron grate over the fire, allowing for nuanced heat control on every plate.

“Monty” — Montague Charcoal Broiler

Made in California, Monty is a French-style enclosed grill that works like a charcoal-fueled oven — perfect for “low and slow” cooking, like a half chicken cooked under a brick. Montague has built grills for over 150 years.

“Nelly” — Mt. St. Helens Shallow-Box Charbroiler

Built by Woodstone in Washington State, Nelly features an additional shallow firebox for coal or wood-fired cooking. She’s named after owner Brad Fredericks’ daughter.
