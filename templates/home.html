Our Story

Real fire. Real flavor.

We burn hardwood charcoal, not gas — it burns cleaner and more evenly than raw wood, sears hotter than a gas flame, and gives every plate that unmistakable backyard-cookout flavor. Friends and family have been meeting on our deck for charcoal-grilled favorites, porch-perfect cocktails, and easy conversation since 2011.
